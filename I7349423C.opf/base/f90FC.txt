O Venerable Ones, the thirty nil)sargika-pāyāntika dharmas have been recited by me. Therefore, I ask the Venerable Ones-A
re you completely pure in this matter?A second and also a third time I ask-Are you completely pure in this matter?Since 
there is silence, the Venerable Ones are completely pure in this matter. Thus do I understand.

The Ninety Pāyantika Dharmas

Now, O Venerable Ones, the ninety pāyāntika dharmas come up in the halfmonthly Prātimokṣa Sūtra recitation.
